Research project workspace for conducting AI-assisted research tasks, including literature review, data analysis, and report generation.

This project uses project-scoped plugins (configured in `.claude/settings.json`):

- **planning-with-files** — Manus-style file-based planning with task_plan.md, findings.md, and progress.md tracking
